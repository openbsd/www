SMP

History and Status:

The project to add SMP support to OpenBSD was started in February, 2000.

As of June, 2004, SMP (Symmetric MultiProcessor) support has been merged into the main OpenBSD development branch. Initially, only the i386 platform supports SMP, but hopefully, others, including amd64, sparc, and sparc64 will ultimately support SMP.

Please see the appropriate platform page for SMP status for your system.
