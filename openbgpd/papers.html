Presentations & Papers

Presentation: 21C3

OpenBGPD and OpenNTPD, Henning Brauer
Presentation on BGPD that was given at 21C3: 21. Chaos Communication Congress in Berlin, Germany in December 2004. These slides were a combined talk introducing both OpenBGPD and OpenNTPD.

Presentation: EuroBSDCon 2004

A Secure BGP Implementation, Henning Brauer
Presentation on BGPD that was given at EuroBSDCon 2004 in Karlsruhe, Germany in October 2004. This presentation outlines implementation details and rationale behind BGPD and is an updated version of the Sucon 04 presentation.

Presentation: SUCON 04

A Secure BGP Implementation, Henning Brauer
Presentation on BGPD that was given at SUCON 04 in Zurich, Switzerland in September 2004. This presentation outlines implementation details and rationale behind BGPD. Tarball of the presentation for offline viewing.

Presentation: FOSDEM 04

Implementing BGP in OpenBSD Henning Brauer
Presentation on BGPD that was given at FOSDEM 2004 in Brussels, Belgium in February 2004. This presentation outlines the process of writing bgpd.
