  • 世界各地 (寄到任何地方) (也可參考以下西歐部份)
    接受訂購 CDROMs的主要地點是 Computer Shot of Calgary(世界上持續經營最久的 線上購物網站). 你可以透過下列各種方式訂購:

  • 透過網路以信用卡訂購:
    • 安全的 SSL 信用卡交易
    • 不安全的 非-SSL 信用卡交易
    請注意信用卡可能以加拿大幣計費, 幣值匯率以中間值計算. 有一些信用卡公司, 針對跨國交易, 會額外收取費用. 請自行斟酌使用較有利的信用卡進行交易. 也 因為每張信用卡, 通常都會有些額外的收費款項, 我們也無法預測正確的總金額.

    (如果你使用 Netscape 3 或是舊版 IE, 你需要 更新 你的 Thawte root certificate 並重新啟動你的瀏覽器).

  • 其他付款方式:
    • PayPal: 訂購款項請寄送給 cshop@canuck.com. 如果你知道包含運費的總價, 比如單一個 CD 包(請看以下的郵件訂購, 或直接向我們詢問), 請直接以網頁訂購, 繳款方式 請選擇預繳方式 "pre-arranged", 並且在該筆訂單的意見欄位中, 註明是經由 PayPal 繳納訂購款項.
    • 銀行匯款轉帳: 在法國, 匯款轉帳可能不需額外收費, 即使有也通常是十歐元, 或者低於一般市場交易的附加費用. 在北美的銀行匯款轉帳 費用是 25 美元. 除非是在法國, 否則我們必須在訂購款項中加上這些費用, 畢竟這 是匯款者所需承擔的費用. 如果要使用銀行匯款轉帳, 請來信向我們詢問.

  • 電話訂購:
    如果要用電話訂購, 請來電 1 (403) 243-4356. 請在一般工作天 的白天時間來電, 以北美山區時間(North American Mountain Time)為準. (有時候下班時間, 也接受來電訂購: 一般工作天晚間的 5PM-9PM, 以及週末的 10AM-9PM, 這段時間不保證有人受理電話訂購.) 請預先準備好你的萬事達, 威士達, 或美國運通信用卡.

    如果你需要直接聯絡 Computer Shop, 請寄 email 到 Austin Hook. 大型機關組織的訂購可以用 傳真, 來向 Computer Shop 下訂單, 傳真電話是 1 (403) 283-2028 .

    我該為訂購物買保險嗎? Items lost in the regular mail are at your risk and expense. You agree to accept our shipping records as the final proof of shipment. However, if a problem does occur, your affidavit that the parcel was not received, plus our shipping records, are generally sufficient to persuade Canada post to refund postage paid. Secondly, under such circumstances, we will replace items lost, at manufacturing cost, which is at most half price, and almost trivial for CD sets and posters. For example a poster gone missing in the mail can be replaced for $2.

    Cost of insurance will vary according to the value of the shipment, except that for small shipments there is a minimum cost of about $7-10 (CDN $ in Canada, US $ elsewhere) - which is why we usually recommend not to insure for small shipments. Moreover, inside USA and Canada, there is a service called (Priority) Express Post, which in addition to trackability and guaranteed faster delivery, includes some insurance. See further discussion below.

    Default insurance for air parcels > 1 kg to USA: Our post office offers default insurance up to $100 CDN (about $65 US) for air parcels weighing more than 1 kg and that means, really, twice or more than that amount, since we replace losses at cost. So for shipments of approximately 5 or more T-shirts (depending on size) or 9 or more CD ROM sets, you can trust there is at least some insurance, since by default we ship by air.

    Default insurance for air parcels > 2 kg sent outside USA/Canada: Our post office offers default insurance of up to $100 CDN (about $65 US) for air parcels weighing more than 2 kg. (comments in previous paragraph apply here too).

    掛號(Registration): 美國及加拿大以外的地區, 我們通常會對 0.2 到 2 公斤 的包裹用掛號寄送. 掛號的費用除了小型空運郵資以外, 需要另加 7 到 10 美元. 掛號費用包括了 40 美元的保險費用. 假如你願意承擔貨運遺失風險, 也可以指定不使 用掛號寄送. 如果你不確定, 那麼你應該指定掛號寄送, 選擇 "please do register". (在後者的狀況下, 我們會依經驗自行判斷, 寄往該國的郵件是否需要使用掛號)

    In USA/Canada less than 1% of items go missing in the mail; it might be yours, yet paying for postal insurance is an expensive gamble. But if you absolutely can't stand risk, do insure by all means. In North America consider specifying Express Mail (trackable), or a courier like FEDEX (also trackable).

    Note that postage cost for a CD set is expected to be around $3 - $5 USD. Packages will generally be mailed from Calgary, Alberta, Canada. (Some US orders are mailed from a US depot -- but unlikely at initial release time). Typical airmail postage rates for quantity 1 are roughly:

    • Canada $3 CDN. Expect 7 days.
    • USA $3 USD ($4 CDN). Expect 7 - 10 days.
    • World $4 USD ($5 CDN). Expect two weeks.
    Note: Transit time estimates do not take into account the fact that shipping backlogs during the first month after a new release may require an extra one to two weeks before the package hits the mail. During the rest of the year turn-around is same day or a couple of days at most.

    快遞郵件 A new service, previously not available, but for Canada and USA only, is Express Post, with 3-6 day delivery, up to $50 insurance included, and trackability from Canadapost This is now an new option on the web order form.

    For a single CD set:

    • Canada $7. Expect 2 - 3 days.
    • USA $10. Expect 4 - 6 days.

    If you want your CD to come very quickly, you can choose a courier like Fedex or UPS overnight. Note, that for single CDs delivered by courier it is cheaper to get overnight delivery than to get 2 or 3 day delivery by the same couriers! If you choose overnight courier service, you very likely do NOT want to use the Internet web ordering system, but rather want to order your package by phoning the Computer Shop phone number (403 243-4356). By phoning before 3PM Mountain Time, you can usually have it by early the next morning!

    If you are ordering the CD plus other items, consider having only the CD sent by overnight and other things by regular airmail or express post. CDs are relatively inexpensive to send by courier (we class them as documents), whereas T-shirts, and especially posters, are quite expensive that way. Sometimes, particularly for posters, we will guess that this is what you want to do. Say in the comments if you really want all items sent by courier, even posters.

    For Canada, and USA only, you may specify "use credit card" in the box where it says courier account number. In that case we will pass on your credit card number to the courier company. You will get a separate debit from them (for the shipping portion only). But we prefer if you do have a courier account number already. If requesting shipping to a company address, ask if they have one that can be used (or phone FEDEX or UPS and ask what it is).

    Typical Fedex or UPS priority shipping rates for quantity 1 are roughly:

    • Canada $13.00 CDN. Overnight if phoned in before 3PM MST.
    • USA $19 USD ($28 CDN). Overnight (to most cities) if phoned in before 3PM MST.
    • World $36 USD ($55.00 CDN) -- 2 to 4 day service. Must have courier account #. If necessary, apply for one or, otherwise, use insured AIR MAIL post for the closest equivalent.

    只運送部份訂購物: 有時候某個尺寸的 T 恤剛好缺貨. 即使你在訂購單中 指定用快遞郵件, 或貨運公司, 我們將不會以你指定的方式, 寄送一開始缺貨的 最後一批訂購物. 如果你想更改這個寄送方式, 請在訂購單的意見欄中, 寫下你 對延遲寄送的訂購物, 較中意的寄送方式.
